Discover Petoskey

Welcome to Petoskey, Michigan – a beautiful place to relax, play golf, and explore. Use the navigation above to check out top golf courses, must-see attractions, and great local restaurants.

🏌️‍♂️ Top Golf Courses

  • Bay Harbor Golf Club – 27-hole resort course with Lake Michigan views.
  • Crooked Tree Golf Club – Hillside course with panoramic scenery.
  • Springbrook Golf Course – Casual 18-hole public course.
  • Petoskey–Bay View Country Club – Historic private course by the bay.

🌲 Local Attractions

  • Petoskey State Park – Dunes, beach, and Petoskey stones.
  • Little Traverse Wheelway – Scenic trail along the bay.
  • Downtown & Gaslight District – Boutiques, galleries, and charm.
  • Mineral Well Park – Quaint park by the river.

🍽️ Dining Experiences

  • Chandler’s – A Restaurant – Upscale American fare.
  • Spring & Porter – Modern fine dining and drinks.
  • The Beacon – Burgers, cocktails, and bay views.
  • Palette Bistro – Wine, bistro dishes, and a patio.
  • The Back Lot Petoskey – Food trucks and beer garden fun.
Enjoy your visit to Petoskey! ⛳
